# Join the MSCA DIGITAL network through collaborations 

# You are a...

## PhD Candidate

Partnering with Aspiring Researchers

We invite PhD candidates of all ages to join our pioneering project, to become part of our innovative European PhD program in Digital Finance. Engage in cutting-edge research, benefit from expert supervision, contribute to groundbreaking studies and get immersed in industry through prestigious secondment opportunities that will shape the future of the digitalization of European and international finance. As a part of our collaborative network, you will have the opportunity to work alongside leading academics, industry experts, gaining valuable experience and advancing your academic career through significant networking and professional opportunities. Until August 2024, you are invited to check the Euraxess page to apply for open positions or check our dedicated webpage (Webpage link). Otherwise, get in touch with us {direct mailto link to someone in charge of continuous recruitment following the initial cohort – when we start to consider that a possibility}

[Contact us ](mailto:msca-digitalfinance@utwente.nl?subject=%5BCollaborate%20on%20Research%2C%20Joerg%20%26%20Marcos%5D)

## University or Research Centre

Academic and Research Partnerships

Universities and research centres are integral to the success of our European PhD program. We seek to establish partnerships with academic institutions that are committed to advancing knowledge, help train the next generation of researchers in topics related to digital finance. By collaborating with us (get in touch with us to schedule a call to discuss partnership possibilities {direct mailto link to someone in charge of continuous recruitment following the initial cohort}) your institution will have the opportunity to co-develop innovative curricula, participate in joint research projects, and host academic events that foster interdisciplinary and international dialogue, discovery and contribution for impact in the industry.

​

[Contact us ](mailto:msca-digitalfinance@utwente.nl?subject=%5BCollaborate%20on%20Training%2C%20Wouter%20%26%20Marcos%5D)

## Company

Industry Engagement and Secondments

​

Companies play an increasingly crucial role in bridging the gap between academic research and real-world application, and that notion is at the very heart of the MSCA DIGITAL network. We invite businesses, institutions, stakeholders to both host secondments and sponsor PhD candidates, providing them with practical experience and insights into industry challenges while ensuring a new frontier-quality and holistic training format, mechanically inducing international networking at its core. By partnering with us, your company will gain access to cutting-edge research, innovative solutions, and the opportunity to help train the next generation of experts in topics related to digital finance. Let us know {direct mailto link to someone in charge of continuous recruitment following the initial cohort} if you have interest to discuss partnership possibilities with our team.

[Contact us ](mailto:msca-digitalfinance@utwente.nl?subject=%5BCollaborate%20on%20Dissemination%2C%20Communication%2C%20and%20Exploitation%2C%20Branka%20%26%20Marcos%5D)

## Media

Dissemination and Outreach

​

Effective communication of our activities, proactive dissemination of our research outputs and more generally engaging with the public are quintessential for spreading the impact our European PhD program already has shown and will continue to foster. We seek non-profit and media partners to help disseminate our research findings, highlight our program’s achievements, and raise awareness about the significance of the topics of MSCA DIGITAL. By collaborating with us, media organizations and non-profits can access exclusive content, expert interviews, and have an overview of the latest developments in this extremely dynamic field.
