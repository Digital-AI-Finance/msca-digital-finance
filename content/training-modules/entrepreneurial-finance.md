### **Entrepreneurial Finance**

  * Leading institution: Bern Business School

  * EC: 1

### General Description

Entrepreneurial Finance' is a course designed to equip doctoral candidates with the essential skills and strategies needed to excel in the realm of financial entrepreneurship. Through a curriculum covering key topics such as financial modeling, valuation, and capital raising, participants will gain the knowledge and confidence to navigate the complexities of entrepreneurial ventures. By fostering an entrepreneurial mindset and leadership qualities, this course enables participants to drive meaningful impact and contribute to positive change in the world of finance.
