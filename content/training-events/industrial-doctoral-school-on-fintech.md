### Industrial Doctoral School on FinTech

  * Leading institution: Poznań University of Economics and Business

  * EC: 4

### General Description

MSCA Digital will organise an open industrial doctoral school on FinTech, tailored to doctoral candidates in digital finance. During this event, fellows will be able to interact with doctoral candidates in digital finance from outside the network. The theme of the doctoral school is “Disrupting Finance with Digital Technologies” and focuses on industrial applications of FinTech.
