### Closing Conference

  * Leading institution: University of Twente

  * EC: 2

### General Description

A selection of renowned keynote speakers from both academia and industry will speak at plenary sessions. Fellows will also have the chance to speak with principal scientists and industrial partners, reflecting on their work. The best project of the doctoral network, selected by the advisory board, will receive a Best Doctoral Research award.
