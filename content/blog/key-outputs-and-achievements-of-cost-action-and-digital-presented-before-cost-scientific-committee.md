# Key Outputs and Achievements of COST Action and DIGITAL presented before COST Scientific Committee

  * ![Writer: Bjorn van Braak](/images/blog/ACg8ocJeSbf9mEFxd10z9gx4zAkJ5kc5ec3slzdv_d2da825e.jpg)

[Bjorn van Braak](/members-area/stephanie-blum/profile/)
  * May 14, 2024
  * 1 min read

Joerg Osterrieder, the Action Chair of COST Action CA19130 and Coordinator of the MSCA Industrial Doctoral Network, highlighted the significant achievements toward the objective of these research projects before the [_COST Scientific Committee_](https://www.cost.eu/about/who-is-who/scientific-committee/).

  

The COST Action network started with 98 FinAI members, 5 MeetUp members, and 21 European countries grew in the course of 4 years to a large European network of 368 FinAI members, 2400 MeetUp members, and 51 European countries. Additionally, the action has partners all around the world, namely in: Australia, Hong Kong, India, Singapore, Taiwan, UAE, United States, Vietnam, New Zealand.

![ree](/images/blog/a607fb_2d6ef27c88e54b8eb8edac7889c5089d_mv2_e2079f0b.png)

  

The Action held over 370 conferences and workshops. A few of last years conferences are highlighted here:

  * [Ethical AI and Data Protection in Finance and Beyond (EAIDP-23) (](https://conference.fin-ai.eu/azores_2023/)[fin-ai.eu](http://fin-ai.eu)[)](https://conference.fin-ai.eu/azores_2023/)

  * [Advanced Statistical Modelling for Fintech, Financial Inclusion and Inequality – Fintech and Artificial Intelligence in Finance (](https://fin-ai.eu/affine/)[fin-ai.eu](http://fin-ai.eu)[)](https://fin-ai.eu/affine/)

  * [COST Action FinAI: Fintech and AI in Finance – Training School (](https://conference.fin-ai.eu/PhD-School-Fintech-and-AI-in-Finance/)[fin-ai.eu](http://fin-ai.eu)[)](https://conference.fin-ai.eu/PhD-School-Fintech-and-AI-in-Finance/)

  * [Women in Fintech 2023 – Fintech and Artificial Intelligence in Finance (](https://fin-ai.eu/womeninfintech2023/)[fin-ai.eu](http://fin-ai.eu)[)](https://fin-ai.eu/womeninfintech2023/)

Additional information about COST Action CA19130 can be found on [_fin-ai.eu_](http://fin-ai.eu)
