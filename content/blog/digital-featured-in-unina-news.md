# DIGITAL featured in UNINA News

  * ![Writer: Branka Hadji-Misheva](/images/blog/AATXAJy16JcE0FkJ6L_EZT3EZBrSKxyZAhDR-WdJ_d2e4fc6f.jpg)

[Branka Hadji-Misheva](https://www.digital-finance-msca.com/members-area/brankahm/profile)
  * Mar 25, 2024
  * 1 min read

DIGITAL, our research and training program in digital finance, is promoted by a consortium of prestigious universities, including the University of Naples Federico II, leading companies, innovative SMEs and important research centres in 11 European countries, together with the European Central Bank. Among the representatives of the University of Naples Federico II, we have Maria Iannario, WP leader and co-coordinator of the Diversity team, Vittorio Amato, Marcella Corduas, Domenico Ciuonzo, Alfoso Iodice d'Enza and Francesco Palumbo.
