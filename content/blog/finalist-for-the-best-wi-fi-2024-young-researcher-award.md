# Finalist for the Best WI-FI 2024 Young Researcher Award

  * ![Writer: Bjorn van Braak](/images/blog/ACg8ocJeSbf9mEFxd10z9gx4zAkJ5kc5ec3slzdv_d2da825e.jpg)

[Bjorn van Braak](/members-area/stephanie-blum/profile/)
  * Jun 11, 2024
  * 1 min read

The MSCA DIGITAL is proud to help organise the contest for the best Young Researcher during the [_WI-FI (Woman in Fintech) 2024 Conference_](https://fin-ai.eu/womeninfintech2024/). The contest, led by DIGITAL's [_Maria Iannario_](/people/maria-iannario/) , focusses on recognizing young researchers (below 40 years of age) leading research projects. Additionally, [_Joerg Osterrieder_](/people/joerg-osterrieder/) and[ _Codruta Mare_](/people/codruta-mare/) serve as jury members for the contest.

  

This year's finalists are:

  * [_Ștefana Belbe (_](https://www.linkedin.com/in/stefana-belbe-cioban-650bb810b/)[_Cioban)_](https://www.linkedin.com/in/stefana-belbe-cioban-650bb810b/) with _Evaluation of the space-time effects of Covid-19 on loans and savings in Romania - A spatial panel data approach at county level_

  * [ _Antonio Cola_](https://www.linkedin.com/in/antonio-cola/) & [_Rosario Urso_](https://www.linkedin.com/in/rosario-urso/) with _Statistical Analysis of FinTech Data: Insights from Unsupervised to Supervised Approaches_

  * [ _Christos Adam_](https://www.linkedin.com/in/christos-adam-b26941275/) with _Segmenting female students' perceptions about Fintech using Explainable AI_

  * [ _Sofia Giantsidi_](https://www.linkedin.com/in/sofia-giantsidi/) with _Optimizing Financial Forecasting: Reservoir Computing and Self-Adaptive Cuckoo-Genetic Algorithm_

  * [ _Stina Morina_](https://www.linkedin.com/in/stina-morina-661501204/) with _Beyond the Screen: Banking Conversational AI_   

The finalists are asked to prepare a speed talk, where they will be assessed based on the quality of the research. The winner will receive an award which includes a fast-tracked review and possible publication of the paper in the Special Issue on _Artificial Intelligence in Finance and Economics,_[ Frontiers in Artificial Intelligence](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/artificial-intelligence). For more information about the contest [Best WI-FI 2024 Young Researcher Paper – FinTech Contest – Fintech and Artificial Intelligence in Finance (](https://fin-ai.eu/best-wi-fi-2024/)[fin-ai.eu](http://fin-ai.eu)[)](https://fin-ai.eu/best-wi-fi-2024/)
