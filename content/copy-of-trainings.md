## Trainings

Our Digital Finance Doctoral Training Programme positions itself at the frontier of academic and industrial innovation in digital finance, blending rigorous foundational courses with specialized digital finance modules, comprehensive soft skills development, and strategic secondment opportunities at leading financial institutes across Europe. Designed to equip future leaders with both theoretical knowledge and practical expertise, our programme offers doctoral candidates across Europe a holistic approach to understanding and shaping the evolving landscape of digital finance. Join us to gain the skills, insights, and real-world experience needed to excel in academia, industry, and beyond.  

The DIGITAL program offers comprehensive doctoral training in four key areas

  * [Training through research and mandatory scientific training](https://www.digital-finance-msca.com/training-through-research-and-mandatory-scientific-training)

  * Advanced scientific training

  * Transferable skills training

  * Training through secondments

Furthermore, the training of doctoral candidates will consist of attending international conferences and other training programs, lab training, and lectures from external scientific lecturers.
