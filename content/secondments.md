## Secondments

Each doctoral candidate will spend a certain amount of time in industry. 

​

The secondments enable the exchange of knowledge between different academic disciplines, different sectors (academia, industry), and different locations and, thereby, increase the researchers’ sets of transferrable skills and improve employability in industry.

All students are hired by different universities within Europe
