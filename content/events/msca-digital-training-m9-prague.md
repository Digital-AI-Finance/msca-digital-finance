# MSCA DIGITAL - TRAINING M9 - PRAGUE

We invite you to attend the STAT of ML - Statistics of Machine learning conference to be held September 30, 2024 in Prague, Czech Republic, hosted by the Czech Academy of Sciences.

![MSCA DIGITAL - TRAINING M9 - PRAGUE](/images/events/11062b_54a8b3b85cfd48f9b560fc3c6fa9b986_mv2_43658353.jpg)

![MSCA DIGITAL - TRAINING M9 - PRAGUE](/images/events/11062b_54a8b3b85cfd48f9b560fc3c6fa9b986_mv2_ad478518.jpg)

## Time & Location

30 Sept 2024, 09:00 – 16:00 CEST

Praha 1, Národní 1009, Staré Město, 110 00 Praha-Praha 1, Czechia

## About the event

**STAT of ML - Statistics of Machine learning** conference to be held September 30, 2024 in Prague, Czech Republic, hosted by the Czech Academy of Sciences.

**Wolgang Karl Härdle, Jozef Barunik and Milos Kopa** will be speaking at during the event. The topics of the conference are Statistics, Machine Learning and Digital Finance.

As both an early [MSCA DIGITAL](https://www.digital-finance-msca.com/)'s work programme training session and a [COST19130 ](https://www.cost.eu/actions/CA19130/)action event, the conference serves both as a research opportunity and as a knowledge dissemination event for _Digital Finance._

**Register on Meetup:**[ MSCA DIGITAL EVENT - TRAINING SESSION M9 - PRAGUE , Fri, Sep 27, 2024, 9:00 AM | Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/fintech_ai_in_finance/events/301751691/?slug=fintech_ai_in_finance&eventId=301751691&isFirstPublish=true)

A call for papers is also available, with relevant information accessible directly on the event [webpage](https://barunik.github.io/Prague2023/blog/call-for-papers).

You are welcome to register your interest [here](https://www.digital-finance-msca.com/contact-us), should you want to also contribute to the network.
