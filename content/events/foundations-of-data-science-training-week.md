# Foundations of Data Science - Training Week

Join us for this 4ECTS doctoral training course "Foundations of Data Science" tailored specifically for doctoral students in finance, providing a comprehensive and practical foundation in data science as applied to financial analysis and decision-making, along with "Project Management" (1ECTS).

![Foundations of Data Science - Training Week](/images/events/d826e4_605d1d993ba84eafbacc6c7739b0e4c2_mv2_6abe6feb.jpg)

![Foundations of Data Science - Training Week](/images/events/d826e4_605d1d993ba84eafbacc6c7739b0e4c2_mv2_1c81d882.jpg)

## Time & Location

20 Mar 2025, 13:00 – 04 Apr 2025, 18:30

Babes-Bolyai University (BBU), Strada Teodor Mihali 58-60, Cluj-Napoca 400591, Romania

## About the event

**Register on Meetup:**

  * For the event on March 20th (1 pm - 2 pm): [_Online Event BBU_](https://www.meetup.com/fintech_ai_in_finance/events/306569378/?utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=group-new-rsvps)

  * For the training week from March 31st - April 4th: [_MSCA Training Week - BBU_](https://www.meetup.com/fintech_ai_in_finance/events/306569487/?utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=group-new-rsvps)

  

The course is tailored specifically for doctoral students in finance, providing a comprehensive

and practical foundation in data science as applied to financial analysis and decision-making.

This course equips participants with the essential skills and knowledge required to effectively

extract, transform, analyze, and model financial data using Python.

  

For better organization, please complete the form at the following link: [_Organization details_](https://forms.gle/X68w2hrLRAYwu7m29)

  

To participate online, use the Zoom invitation link below: [_Zoom participation_](https://econ-ubbcluj-ro.zoom.us/j/81658602430)

  

Upon completion, doctoral students will not only be proficient in technical data science skills but

also adept at applying these skills within the context of finance. They will be capable of

conducting high-level financial analysis, developing predictive models, and translating data-

driven insights into actionable strategies. This course bridges the gap between data science and

financial theory, preparing students to lead and innovate in the finance industry through rigorous

data-driven methodologies.
