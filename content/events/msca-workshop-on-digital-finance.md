# MSCA - Workshop on Digital Finance

The Bucharest University of Economic Studies (ASE) is pleased to invite you to participate in the Digital Finance Workshop, scheduled for January 15-17, 2025. The workshop will be held at the Bucharest University of Economic Studies, located at Piața Romană 6, Bucharest.

![MSCA - Workshop on Digital Finance](/images/events/1cd49e_479dde14ec704782a57c0046cce006da_mv2_4bea35ec.png)

![MSCA - Workshop on Digital Finance](/images/events/1cd49e_479dde14ec704782a57c0046cce006da_mv2_7ae773fb.png)

## Time & Location

15 Jan 2025, 10:00 EET – 17 Jan 2025, 18:00 EET

Room Virgil Magdearu (Ground Floor), Academia de Studii Economice, Clădirea Ion N. Angelescu, Piața Romană 6, București 010371, Romania

## About the event

This workshop, organized through a collaboration between [MSCA DIGITAL FINANCE](https://www.digital-finance-msca.com/), [IDA Institute Digital Assets,](https://www.theida.net/) [AI4EFin](https://www.ai4efin.ase.ro/), and the [Institute for Economic Forecasting of the Romanian Academy](https://ipe.ro/new/), will feature presentations and discussions on cutting-edge topics in Digital Finance.

**Event Details:**

  * **Date:** January 15-17, 2025

  * **Location:** Bucharest University of Economic Studies, Piața Romană 6, Building Ion Angelescu, Room Virgil Magdearu (Ground Floor, Main Entrance)

  * **Format:** The event will feature presentations from industry experts and researchers on recent developments in the field of digital finance, particularly focusing on AI-driven solutions for financial markets, blockchain, digital currencies, and economic forecasting using digital tools.

  

**MSCA DIGITAL FINANCE PhD students and partners are especially encouraged to participate** , as this workshop will provide an excellent opportunity for networking, knowledge exchange, and collaboration on ongoing projects within the field of digital finance.

  

**Registration:**

Please use the following [**link**](https://forms.gle/SgefUkLDGmkfkc2x9)**** to register, by November 15, 2024.

Participants are encouraged to register as early as possible to secure their spot, as space is limited.

Please note that participants are responsible for arranging their own accommodation, meals, and personal expenses during the program.
