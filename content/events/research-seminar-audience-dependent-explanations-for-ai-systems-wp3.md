# Research Seminar: Audience Dependent Explanations for AI Systems (WP3)

## About the event

The WP3 meeting and research seminar includes a research talk by **Patricia Evite** , who is working on Audience Dependent Explanations for AI Systems.

  

**Link to participate:** [**_Join the meeting now_**](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/meetup-join/19%3ameeting_ODI4NGQ4NmQtYTI2YS00OTZhLTg0ZDMtYjQwZGRkNzEzNDMy%40thread.v2/0?context=%7b%22Tid%22%3a%22d6a1cf8c-768e-4187-a738-b6e50c4deb4a%22%2c%22Oid%22%3a%22db40811d-87e1-4a50-8210-833144058d51%22%7d)

  

###  Speaker

  

![ree](/images/events/d826e4_24004ffefd784f62b8d1665308ffea1f_mv2_17ff9baf.png)

  

**Patricia Evite** is a doctoral candidate at the University of Naples Federico II within the MSCA Doctoral Network for Digital Finance. She holds a bachelor’s degree in economics and a master's degree in economics for the global transition. Her Individual Research Project (IRP9) focuses on audience-dependent explanations.
