# Research Seminar: Fair Algorithmic Design (WP3)

## Time & Location

03 Sept 2025, 11:00 – 12:00

## About the event

The WP3 meeting and research seminar includes a research talk by **Rebecca Di Francesco** , who is working on Fair Algorithmic Design.

  

**Link to participate:** [**_Join the meeting now_**](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/meetup-join/19%3ameeting_ODViNmNlNzAtNmFiMy00MTJjLWIxY2ItZTc1YWZhN2E2YWE4%40thread.v2/0?context=%7b%22Tid%22%3a%22d6a1cf8c-768e-4187-a738-b6e50c4deb4a%22%2c%22Oid%22%3a%22db40811d-87e1-4a50-8210-833144058d51%22%7d)

  

###  Speaker

  

![ree](/images/events/3b5556_92afbbe565ac4b9fb476dc85a94fe136_mv2_3839e867.png)

  

**Rebecca Di Francesco** is a doctoral candidate at the Bern University of Applied Sciences within the MSCA Doctoral Network for Digital Finance. She holds a bachelor’s degree in economics and a master's degree in data science. Her Individual Research Project (IRP17) focuses on fair algorithmic design and portfolio optimization under sustainability concerns.
