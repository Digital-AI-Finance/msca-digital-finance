## About the event

The seminar is organized within the framework of WP5 activities and will cover advanced statistical methods for decision-making under uncertainty. It is aimed at Master's and PhD students interested in the study of models for ordinal data, which are commonly used in evaluation processes.

###  Speaker

![ree](/images/events/d826e4_184a15eeeab749738db8e2a9654d435d_mv2_4b968500.jpg)

  

**Maria Iannario** is an Associate Professor in Statistics at the University of Naples Federico II and leads work package 5 of the MSCA Digital project. The main results of her work consist of the study and development of a class of statistical models used for the analysis of ordinal data.
