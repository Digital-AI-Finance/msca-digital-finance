# MSCA - Midterm Report Meeting @UTW

2 Days dedicated to the Midterm Report Meeting

![MSCA - Midterm Report Meeting @UTW](/images/events/e64bca_9dc7d79d8f124aecbb5e2ce89c7c5e64_mv2_f6d78ba6.jpg)

![MSCA - Midterm Report Meeting @UTW](/images/events/e64bca_9dc7d79d8f124aecbb5e2ce89c7c5e64_mv2_ba326e7c.jpg)

## Time & Location

20 May 2025, 07:30 – 21 May 2025, 16:00

Enschede, Ravelijn, Hallenweg 17, 7522 NH Enschede, Netherlands

## About the event

**Tuesday (20/05)**

MSCA Research Day

  * 9:00-10:30 – Each WP present future plans & Future Research, call to collaborate

    * WP1- 9:00-9:15

    * WP2- 9:15-9:30

    * WP3- 9:30-9:45

    * WP4- 9:45-10:00

    * WP5- 10:00-10:15

    * WP9- 10:15-10:30

  * 10:30-10:45 - 15min break

  * 10:45-12:00 - 3 min standup (17 PhDs): To have an one-slide and do a 3-minute thesis [Do select winner (by scientific committee) + peoples choice --- award/certificate] 

  * 12:00-13:00 - Lunch

  * 13:00 – 17:00 – Round Table to create/define interdisciplinary topics (WPs) & to create their pair from ppl from different WPs to work on the problems & Define goal to start collaboration/ research + Writing with Supervisors

    * 13:00-14:30 – Round Table

    * 14:30-15:00 – Break

    * 15:00-16:00 – Defining Pairs, define goals, start defining research questions & writing session

**Wednesday (21/05)**

Midterm report meeting

  *     * [Agenda](https://universiteittwente.sharepoint.com/:w:/r/sites/MSCADIGITAL/Gedeelde%20documenten/MSCA%20Coordination/Continuous%20Reporting/Midterm%20Meeting/HE-MSCA-DN_midterm_meeting_COO_guidelines.docx?d=w00b7d028b12b4cda95fe3b7bce51f24d&csf=1&web=1&e=EmNYkQ) reference

    * 09:00 – 09:15 | Welcome & Introduction (REA Officer, Coordinator)

    * 09:15 – 09:45 | Tour de Table (Scientists-in-Charge, Associated Partners)

    * 09:45 – 10:05 | REA Officer Presentation (Monitoring, Recruitment, Reporting)

    * 10:05 – 11:05 | Coordinator’s Report (Recruitment, Deliverables, Risks, Open Science)

    * 11:05 – 11:20 | Coffee Break

    * 11:20 – 12:30 | DC Presentations (Research, Training, Secondments)

    * 12:30 – 13:30 | Lunch Break

    * 13:30 – 14:30 | DCs Restricted Session (Supervision, Training, Career)

    * 14:30 – 14:45 | Coordinator & REA Officer Restricted Session

    * 14:45 – 15:15 | Feedback & Discussion (REA Officer)

    * 15:15 – 15:30 | Closing Remarks (Coordinator, REA Officer)
