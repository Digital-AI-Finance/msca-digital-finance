Location is Hybrid

## About the event

**Supervisory Board Meeting#3** Hybrid format, in Naples *University of Naples, Federico II and online (link already provided by mail or upon request)

_Consists of:_

  * One representative of each partner - UTW, BBU, UNA, CAR, ASE, WWU, KUT, ROY, ARC, EIT, FRA, DEL, SWE, RAI, ECB

  * All supervisors - two supervisors from each partner

  * All WP leaders - BBU, WWU, BFH, UNA, UTW

  * Two annually rotating doctoral candidates

  * Diversity coordinator - Codruta Mare

  * Co-chaired by the project coordinator and the EU representative - Jörg Osterrieder and Gergana Simeonova-Arida

 _It is responsible for:_

  * Approving and supervising the financial, marketing, and communication strategies

  * Monitoring and evaluating the progress of training and research milestones

  * Serving as an internal audit team for the EB when it decides on budget allocation, organisational structure, and consortium strategy
