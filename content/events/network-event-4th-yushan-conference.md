## About the event

**Welcome to the 4th Yushan Conference**! It is an international conference on digital finance, aimed at bringing together professionals from academia and industry to facilitate dialogue and collaboration. Join us to learn about the latest advancements in digital finance and network with leading experts in the field.

It is in alignment with Taiwan's Ministry of Education Yushan Scholar Fellow Program, dedicated to enhancing research cooperation and fostering global research integration.

  

Conference website : [The 4th Yushan Conference (notion.site)](https://yushan-conference.notion.site/The-4th-Yushan-Conference-9e9d4beb98e64e5c928e5e0680c80d67)

  

Conference speakers :

[Ioana Florina Coita](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ioana-florina-coita-72b4a626/?originalSubdomain=ro) (University of Oradea, Romania)

[Stefan Lessmann](https://www.wiwi.hu-berlin.de/en/Professorships/bwl/wi/personen-en/hl/standardseite-en) (Humboldt University of Berlin, Germany)

[Chuan-Ju Wang](http://cfda.csie.org/~cjwang/) (Academia Sinica, Taiwan)

[Mark L. Shope](https://markshope.com/) (National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University, Taiwan)

  

Conference venue : **National Taipei University of Technology, Taipei** & **National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University, Hsinchu**
