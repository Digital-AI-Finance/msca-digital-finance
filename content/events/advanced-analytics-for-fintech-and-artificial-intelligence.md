# Advanced Analytics for Fintech and Artificial Intelligence

## Time & Location

21 Oct 2024, 09:30 – 14:30 CEST

Aula Dainelli, UNINA, Largo S. Marcellino, 80138 Napoli NA, Italy

## About the event

The Advanced Analytics for Fintech and Artificial Intelligence conference will close the study cycle of the Blended Intensive Programme Methods for Fintech and Artificial Intelligence in Finance. It was created to foster methodological debate on the contribution of statistics in the fields of Fintech and Artificial Intelligence and Sustainability of Digital Finance within the WP5 of MSCA Industrial Doctoral Network on Digital Finance.

  

The scientific programme includes invited and contributed sessions. Statistical topics of interest include: graphical models, categorical data analysis, statistical models for financial analysis, latent variable models, longitudinal data, MCMC methods, marginal models, non-parametric statistics, time series analysis, biostatistics, Big Data Analytics, high-dimensionality models and computation, statistical process monitoring.
