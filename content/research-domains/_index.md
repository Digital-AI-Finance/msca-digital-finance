The DIGITAL network is structured around five key research work packages, each addressing critical challenges and opportunities in digital finance. Our interdisciplinary approach brings together expertise in finance, computer science, data science, and regulatory compliance to advance the frontier of financial technology.

## Work Package 1: European Financial Data Space

Building robust infrastructure for financial data sharing across Europe through advanced data quality methodologies, augmentation techniques, and standardization frameworks. This work package focuses on creating accessible, high-quality datasets that enable cutting-edge research and innovation in financial services.

**Key Topics:**
- Data quality assessment and improvement
- Data augmentation and synthetic data generation
- Privacy-preserving data sharing technologies
- Financial data standardization and interoperability

## Work Package 2: AI in Financial Markets

Applying state-of-the-art machine learning and deep learning techniques to trading, risk management, portfolio optimization, and market prediction. This work package explores how artificial intelligence can enhance decision-making processes across financial markets while addressing practical implementation challenges.

**Key Topics:**
- Algorithmic trading and market making
- Risk modeling and prediction
- Portfolio optimization using ML
- High-frequency trading analysis
- Market microstructure and price discovery

## Work Package 3: Explainable and Fair AI

Making artificial intelligence transparent, interpretable, and fair in financial applications to build trust, ensure accountability, and meet regulatory compliance requirements. This work package addresses the critical need for AI systems that can explain their decisions and treat all stakeholders equitably.

**Key Topics:**
- Explainable AI (XAI) methods for finance
- Algorithmic fairness and bias detection
- Model interpretability techniques
- Regulatory compliance and AI governance
- Trust and transparency in financial AI

## Work Package 4: Digital Innovation and Blockchain

Exploring decentralized finance (DeFi), cryptocurrencies, smart contracts, and distributed ledger technology for transforming financial services. This work package investigates how blockchain and related technologies can create new financial instruments, improve efficiency, and enable novel business models.

**Key Topics:**
- Blockchain infrastructure and scalability
- DeFi protocols and applications
- Cryptocurrency market analysis
- Smart contracts and tokenization
- Central Bank Digital Currencies (CBDCs)
- Digital assets and NFTs

## Work Package 5: Sustainable Digital Finance

Integrating environmental, social, and governance (ESG) criteria with digital technologies to promote sustainable finance and responsible investment. This work package examines how fintech innovations can accelerate the transition to a more sustainable and inclusive financial system.

**Key Topics:**
- ESG data analysis and rating systems
- Green fintech solutions
- Climate risk modeling
- Sustainable investment strategies
- Impact measurement and reporting
- Regulatory frameworks for sustainable finance

---

Each work package is supported by multiple PhD positions and involves collaboration between academic partners, industry practitioners, and regulatory bodies. Our doctoral candidates benefit from cross-training opportunities, secondments, and participation in all five research domains, fostering a truly interdisciplinary skill set.
