## About

Ruben is a PhD student in finance at the Faculty of Economics and Business Administration within Babeș-Bolyai University. He holds a bachelor's degree in business management, specializing in firm economics, and a master's degree in accounting, specializing in accounting expertise and audit. His greatest passion is mathematics.

### Research Interests

![Finance graduates_edited.jpg](/images/people/Finance_graduates_edited_74e480cf.jpg)
