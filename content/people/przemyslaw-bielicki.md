## About

Przemysław has over 25 years of experience in the financial services industry. He is a member of the investment committees of the funds managed by Royalton Partners and member of the boards of portfolio companies engaged in infrastructure, real estate, retail and IT. Prior to joining Royalton Partners in 1997, Przemysław was the CEO of W. Frąckowiak & Partners, now F5, an M&A and management consulting firm based in Poland. He served as a non-executive board member of several construction companies. Przemysław started his career as a civil engineer in the construction industry and holds a Master of Science degree in Civil Engineering from the Technical University of Poznań and an MBA from INSEAD, France.
