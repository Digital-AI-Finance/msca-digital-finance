## About

Stefan is currently working on the development of a comprehensive Risk Index for Cryptocurrencies, which serves as the core focus of his research at Poznan University of Economics and Business. Alongside this, he manages the [_Quantlet platform,_](https://quantlet.com/) promoting transparent and reproducible coding practices for the research community.

  

With a background in Economic Informatics (Bachelor's) and Statistics and Data Science (Master's), Stefan is eager to expand his expertise and connect with professionals across disciplines.
