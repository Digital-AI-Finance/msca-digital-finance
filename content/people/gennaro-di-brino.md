## About

Gennaro di Brino is currently serving as the Head of Data Science at Cardo AI, leading technical development and supervision of advanced ML models employed by the company. Gennaro's education and early career are marked by notable affiliations with prestigious institutions such as the University of Luxembourg (Marie Curie Fellow), at the Institut des Hautes Études Scientifiques (visiting researcher) and the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics (Postdoctoral Fellow). Before joining Cardo AI, Gennaro has made significant contributions to the field of mathematics through his research. With a focus on pure mathematics, algebraic geometry, and algebraic topology, his publications in several areas of mathematics demonstrate expertise and deep understanding of complex mathematical theories.
