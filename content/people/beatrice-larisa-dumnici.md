## About

Beatrice Larisa is a PhD student in finance at the Faculty of Economic Sciences and Business Administration, Babeş-Bolyai University. Her primary areas of study include green finance, sustainability and business performance. 

  

In 2022, she became a trainee at The Body of Expert and Licensed Accountants of Romania (CECCAR), a well-known, highly reputed professional accounting organization, and one of the largest professional institutes in South-Eastern Europe. In the business environment, she works as an economic director. Along these lines she is working on numerous financial and economic cases.
