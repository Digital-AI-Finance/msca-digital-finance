## About

Tomina-Maria is a first-year PhD student in finance at the Faculty of Economic Sciences and Business Administration of Babeș-Bolyai University. Her academic and professional journey reflects a profound passion for accounting, auditing, and research. She has successfully completed both her bachelor's and master's degrees in accounting and auditing. Additionally, she has accumulated three years of experience in internal banking audit, during which she gained valuable insights into the functioning of financial systems and contributed to ensuring the integrity and efficiency of their processes. .
