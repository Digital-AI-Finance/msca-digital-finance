## About

Stijn van der Pol is a masters student in Industrial Engineering and Management, specializing in Financial Engineering and Management. In addition to his academic pursuits, he has been actively involved at the University of Twente (UT) in coordinating the MSCA. Alongside Julius Kooistra, he has played a key role in facilitating the groundwork for this program and ensuring its trajectory aligns with the intended course. This responsibility involves overseeing the program and its network at an organizational level. Stijn is tasked with managing various aspects, including strategic planning, stakeholder engagement, and resource management, all aimed at guaranteeing the program's successful launch and its consistent adherence to rigorous academic standards.
