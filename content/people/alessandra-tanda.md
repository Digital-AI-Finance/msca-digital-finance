## About

I am currently associate professor in Banking and Finance at the Department of Economics and Management, University of Pavia. I have experience in teaching financial markets and banking both at the undergraduate and post-graduate levels. My main research interests include FinTech, the financial structure of financial and non-financial firms, and the ESG and sustainable profile of financial markets, with special reference to the corporate governance of financial intermediaries. I have published nationally and internationally and taken part in different research projects, including FINTECH HO2020, PERISCOPE and Cost Fin-AI all funded by the EU.
