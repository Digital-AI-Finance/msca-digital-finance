## About

Frédérik Sinan Bernard is the project manager of the MSCA DIGITAL project. 

  

He is a researcher with a transdisciplinary profile (Law, International Relations, Business and Strategy) who finished his PhD in 2022 at the Centre d'Etudes Diplomatiques et Stratégiques (CEDS) in Paris, exploring the intersection of crypto-economics and national economic intelligence systems. He now pursues the academic career he started in 2012 in Cyprus International University as a lecturer. 

  

He developed the MPA program "Geopolitics of Crypto-Economics" at CEDS, first of its kind. He also acquired various experiences in the private sector. He is involved in non-profits (core member of Paris Blockchain Society, fellow for Turkey of Blockchain for Good, mentor in Baslangic Noktasi). 

  

### Key Achievements and Outputs

\- He was invited as a visiting postdoctoral fellow at Oxford University's _**Future of Fintech Initiative**_

\- He has been awarded with a postdoctoctoral position of the _**MSCA DIGITAL**_**** project and subsequently asked to also take on the role of its project and research manager.
