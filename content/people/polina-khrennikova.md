## About

Her research is embracing human decision making and information processing under uncertainty and risk. Her current research interests are in the area of behavioural finance and investments. 

  

Some of her current research projects are: decision-making and risk preferences in investment related choices, agents' expectations and asset price formation, mathematical models of contextual preferences. 

  

In 2019 she received a BA/Leverhulme Small Research Grant to conduct experiments related to financial decision making.
