## About

Vasile Alecsandru Strat is a PhD associate professor at the Department of Statistics and Econometrics at the Faculty of Cybernetics, Statistics and Economic Informatics within the Bucharest University of Economic Studies, while also occupying the position of Dean of the Bucharest Business School. Vasile Alecsandru Strat obtained his Ph.D. in Applied Statistics (2012) from the same university and currently teaches business statistics classes both at undergraduate and MBA level. He has been team member of several nationally and internationally funded research projects and his profile is that of a quantitative researcher in different business areas.
