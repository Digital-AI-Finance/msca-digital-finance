## About

Dr. Axel Groß-Klußmann joined Quoniam in July 2012 and currently serves as a senior research analyst in the Research Forecasts team where he focuses on top-down approaches in Equities and Multi Asset. Axel's educational background includes degrees in Statistics at Humboldt University, Berlin, Quantitative Economics at CAU Kiel, and Maths at the University Hagen. He completed his doctoral studies in financial econometrics at the Humboldt, in collaboration with Deutsche Bank’s former Quantitative Products Laboratory.
