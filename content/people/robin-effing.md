## About

Already in his youth Robin got interested in computer technology and its great power to reshape our lives. 

  

His role at this university is Assistant Professor Business Information Systems at University of Twente, Enschede. 

  

His area of expertise includes (but is not limited to) digital intelligence, big data, digital strategies, social media, smart cities, digital transformation, social change, non profit community innovation, big data, e-participation and political participation. The great thing of his job is that he can never stop learning. Can you?
