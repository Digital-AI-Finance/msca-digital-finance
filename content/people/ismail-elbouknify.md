## About

Ismail ELBOUKNIFY is a PhD student at the University of Naples Federico II, conducting research as part of the MSCA Industrial Doctoral Network on Digital Finance. His project, titled "Experimenting with Green AI to Reduce Processing Time and Contribute to a Low-Carbon Economy", focuses on leveraging advanced statistical modeling and Green AI to tackle critical challenges in sustainable finance. Ismail's research delves into innovative applications of AI in finance, emphasizing strategies to make AI processes more energy-efficient and environmentally friendly. With a strong foundation in artificial intelligence, he has contributed significantly to projects spanning healthcare, education, and finance.
