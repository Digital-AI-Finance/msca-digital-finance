## About

Ronald Hochreiter is Associate Professor at the Research Department of Computational Methods at the WU Vienna University of Economics and Business. He is the president of the Academy of Data Science in Finance, vice-president of the Austrian Society of Operations Research, and senior member of the Association for Computing Machinery. He leads various European research projects, e.g. the WP "AI for Financial Markets" of the EU MSCA Doctoral Network on Digital Finance. Furthermore, he is actively providing consulting on AI for business and management for various companies.
