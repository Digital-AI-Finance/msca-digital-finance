## About

Lennart Baals is a Ph.D. student in quantiative finance and risk management with a particular research interest in blockchain-based finance and network-based credit risk modeling in P2P lending markets. Prior to joining the Ph.D. programme, Lennart achieved University degrees at the undergraduate and post graduate level from two different Eurpoean Universities. He holds a bachelors degree in business administration from the University of Mannheim, Germany, and a masters degree in Finance from Trinity College Dublin.
