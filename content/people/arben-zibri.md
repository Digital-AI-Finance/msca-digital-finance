## About

Arben Zibri serves as the Product Manager for the Data Science division at Cardo AI, where he leads the articulation of product vision. In this capacity, he is responsible for identifying emerging opportunities and application scenarios that can be addressed through methodologies in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence within the realm of structured finance. Prior to his affiliation with Cardo AI, Arben occupied the role of Director of Model Control for Interest Rates and Credit Derivatives at Morgan Stanley. Earlier in his professional trajectory, he undertook various roles in project management and commercial lending. 

  

In addition to his professional commitments, Arben serves as an adjunct faculty member, teaching finance-related courses at EPOKA University and the University of New York in Tirana.
