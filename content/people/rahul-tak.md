## About

Rahul is a MSCA Digital PhD student at the Bucharest University of Economic Studies. He is working on developing industry ready (robust, explainable, transparent, scalable and practical) deep learning based automated trading systems. 

  

He was a consultant in analytics and financial risk management division at Deloitte, where he worked with some of the world’s largest financial institutions to provide them with AI/ML based solutions to prevailing problems in the financial sector like climate change, privacy and forecasting. 

  

He pursued a BS-MS with specialization in Physics from Indian Institute of Science Education and Research. He is interested in building data driven learning methods to understand and study the evolution of Complex Dynamic systems. He is an enthusiastic learner constantly improving understanding of fundamental and emerging topics like ML, XAI, advanced AI algorithms and time series analysis.
