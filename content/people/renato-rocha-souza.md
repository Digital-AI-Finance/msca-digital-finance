## About

Renato is an accomplished professional with a PhD in Information Science, a Master's in Production Engineering, and a Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering. Since 2010, they have excelled as a Faculty Professor, Data Scientist, and Researcher, specializing in Computer Science, Data Science, and Applied Mathematics. His international academic experience includes postdoc positions at renowned institutions such as the University of South Wales, Columbia University, and the Austrian Academy of Sciences. 

  

Currently, he is the Head of the Data Science Academy at Raiffeisen Bank International in Vienna and a lecturer at Universität Wien. With expertise in Data Science, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Digital Humanities, Renato is proficient in Python, SQL, and numerous data science frameworks. He has contributed to open-source projects and hold multiple certifications in machine learning and AI.
