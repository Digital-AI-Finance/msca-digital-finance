## About

Rebecca holds a Bachelor's degree in Economics from the University of Bologna and a Master's degree in Data Science from the University of Padova. After completing her Master's, she worked in the industry for two years before deciding to pursue a PhD, driven by her initial passion for research. Rebecca is currently part of the MSCA (Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions) Digital Finance Doctoral Network. She is passionate about exploring innovative projects at the intersection of AI and finance, which she sees as the perfect synergy with her background. Through her research, Rebecca aims to promote sustainability and fairness within the financial sector.
