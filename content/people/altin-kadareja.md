## About

Altin Kadareja is the CEO and co-founder of Cardo AI, an investment intelligence SaaS/IaaS solution provider supporting institutional investors and credit originators to manage private debt investments through a next generation technology and data science, bringing efficiency and intelligence to middle and back office operations of such market players.  
  
Altin has covered several investment and risk management roles at BlackRock, Prometeia, Intesa Sanpaolo and Allianz Bank Financial Advisors. He holds a master of science degree in Economics and Management of Innovation and Technology from Bocconi University in Milan and followed an executive program in Risk Management at Imperial Business School in London.
