## About

Julius Kooistra is a master student in Business and Information Technology, specializing in Data Science and Business and Enterprise Architecture and IT Management. He is currently working on publishing a paper titled “The Implications of Adversarial Attacks on Shifted Window Transformers in an Eight-Class Breast Cancer Classification Problem”. In addition to his academic pursuits, he has been actively involved at the University of Twente (UT) in coordinating the MSCA. Alongside Stijn van der Pol, he has played a key role in facilitating the groundwork for this program and ensuring its trajectory aligns with the intended course. This responsibility involves overseeing the program and its network at an organizational level. Julius is tasked with managing various aspects, including strategic planning, stakeholder engagement, and resource management, all aimed at guaranteeing the program's successful launch and its consistent adherence to rigorous academic standards.
