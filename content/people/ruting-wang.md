## About

Ruting Wang is a postdoctoral at City University of Hong Kong, HK SAR, China, and Sun Yat-sen University, China. Her research focuses on financial risk, crypto markets, corporate finance, climate change, and capital reallocation. She publish articles in journals as Emerging Markets Review and Quantitative Finance. She has a background in Finance from Sun Yat-sen University for PhD degree, and investigates systemic risks through advanced methodologies such as Financial Risk Meters.
