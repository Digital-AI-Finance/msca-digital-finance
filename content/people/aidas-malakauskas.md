## About

Aidas Malakauskas, holding a doctorate in economics, is a seasoned professional in the finance industry, with a specialization in financial technologies, automation, and artificial intelligence. His research primarily focuses on understanding the challenges associated with access to credit for small and medium enterprises, as well as the utilization of artificial intelligence tools in economic contexts. Currently, Aidas serves as the Head of Transformation and Innovation at Swedbank Baltics, AS, where his responsibilities include spearheading the automation of credit decision-making processes and implementing AI solutions in day-to-day operations.
