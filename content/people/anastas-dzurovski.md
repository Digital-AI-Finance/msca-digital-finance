## About

Full-time professor at the University of Tirana since 2000. Publications and contributions in reputable international scientific conferences and journals. Co-author of academic textbooks in Financial Statement Analysis and Managerial Accounting. Visiting professor at the University of Turin, University of Poitiers and University of Tuscia. Member of the Internal Audit Committee at the Albanian Ministry of Finance; external consultant for the Albanian Power Corporation and the National Regulatory Authority of Albania; external expert for the Agency of Quality for Higher Education in Albania, member of Horizon 2020 and COST projects. Full member of the International Association of Accounting Educators and Researchers.
