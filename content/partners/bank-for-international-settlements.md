### Bank for International Settlements (BIS)

### General Description

The Bank for International Settlements (BIS) is an international financial institution that serves as a central bank for central banks, promoting global monetary and financial stability. It provides a forum for central banks to collaborate, conducts research, and offers financial services to assist in the management of foreign reserves.

### Our Contribution to the MSCA

The BIS contributes macro-economic datasets, assists in ongoing projects, and provides overall expertise in banking supervision.
