### University of Pavia

### General Description

The University of Pavia is a student-centered campus, with more than 26,000 enrolled students, 21 university colleges where boys and girls live and grow together, exchange ideas and projects, and prepare for their future. To these young people, the University of Pavia guarantees a personalized orientation path, with over 400 collaboration agreements with foreign universities and over 700 Erasmus agreements, 3300 internship opportunities, contacts with companies, and the world of work.
