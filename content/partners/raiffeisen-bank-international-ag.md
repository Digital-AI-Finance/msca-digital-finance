### Raiffeisen Research / Raiffeisen Bank International

### General Description

Raiffeisen Research is a diverse team of around 80 analysts based in Austria and 13 countries of Central and Eastern Europe (CEE). Their products and expertise rely on continuous and close collaboration between the research teams in CEE and Vienna. Moreover, they are the exclusive provider to the Austrian Raiffeisen Banking Group regarding retail research.
