### University of Kaiserslautern-Landau (UKL)

### General Description

Officially known as RPTU (Rheinland-Pfälzische Technische Universität Kaiserslautern-Landau), the University of Kaiserslautern-Landau is a German public research university with a focus on engineering, natural sciences, and social sciences. It offers a wide range of academic programs and is recognized for its strong emphasis on interdisciplinary research and innovation.
