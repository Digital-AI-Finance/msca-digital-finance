### Poznań University of Economics and Business

### General Description

The Poznań University of Economics and Business, PUEB (Uniwersytet Ekonomiczny w Poznaniu, UEP) is one of the biggest public universities oriented towards business, economics, management in Poland. Our institution is accredited by AMBA, CEEMAN IQA, CEPI and ACCA. PUEB places 3rd in economics and business oriented universities in Poland (Perspektywy rating), and among world top 200 business schools in the Eduniversal rating. In 2022/23 academic year, PUEB participated in 72 research projects, on national and international scale, as well as providing research services and expertise to public and private bodies. PUEB employs over 500 teachers and trainers, including 140 professors.
