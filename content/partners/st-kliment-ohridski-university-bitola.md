### St. Kliment Ohridski University Bitola N.Macedonia

### General Description

St. Kliment Ohridski University Bitola was founded in 1979. It is the second largest university in North Macedonia, providing first, second, and third cycle courses in social, natural and technical sciences. It is an internationally open university willing to embrace changes in science as well as in society.
