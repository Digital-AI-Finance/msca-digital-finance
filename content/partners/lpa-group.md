### LPA Group

### General Description

LPA transforms the way financial markets worldwide work. The organization offsets their clients’ workload by synergizing software, automation and emerging technology, providing a dynamic and innovative financial services ecosystem. LPA offers service on Software, Management Service and Consulting.   
  
Software: Document and workflow automation technology specifically for financial institutions. Accelerating digital transformation process with configurable software modules that fit seamlessly into existing digital infrastructures.  
  
Managed Services: Streamline, automate and outsource the creation and process of document generation, issuance and reporting with the LPA cloud application, providing an efficient end-to-end service that meets compliance requirements.   
  
Consulting: Accelerating financial market’s transformation with best-in-class domain expertise and cutting-edge solutions, enabling clients in the financial world to remain agile and efficient in complex, dynamic environments.
