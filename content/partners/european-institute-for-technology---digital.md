EIT Digital (EITD)

General Description

EIT Digital is a leading European digital innovation and entrepreneurial education organization driving Europe's digital transformation. EIT Digital has headquarters in Brussels and presence in more than 10 EU countries.

Research: Strongly involved in research and development thanks to a partnership ecosystem including more than 50 organizations among universities, research centres and RTOs .

Training: Training courses on Innovation and Entrepreneurship, Business Planning, Business Development, and Digital Technologies (e.g. Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain).

Industry cooperation: Strong industry connections thanks to a partnership ecosystem including more than 200 industry partners (large corporates or SMEs).
