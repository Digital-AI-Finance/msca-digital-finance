### Deloitte Consulting S.r.l.S.B

### General Description

Deloitte provides industry-leading audit, consulting, tax, and advisory services to many of the world's most admired brands. Analytics and AI services are designed to meet such a wide range of needs.   
  
The team is made up of data scientists, data architects, business and domain specialists, visualization and design specialists, and of course technology and application engineers. Deloitte employs and holds 27,000+ practitioners, 28 delivery centres, 450+ industry-leading reusable assets, and 300+ Ecosystem & Alliances partnerships.
