### G-Research

### General Description

G-Research is a leading quantitative research and technology firm based in London. It applies advanced machine learning, statistical modelling, and big data analytics to forecast movements in global financial markets. Combining academic-level research with cutting-edge engineering, G-Research develops systematic trading strategies and fosters collaborations with universities through sponsorships, research prizes, and academic outreach initiatives.
