### University of Tirana

### General Description

The University of Tirana (UT), founded in 1957, is the largest and most prestigious public university in Albania. Located in the capital city, Tirana, it offers a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs across various fields, including economics, law, social sciences, natural sciences, and humanities.  
  
UT is known for its strong academic tradition, research contributions, and collaboration with European and international institutions. As a key center for higher education and innovation in Albania, the university plays a crucial role in shaping the country's intellectual and professional landscape while promoting academic excellence and internationalization.
