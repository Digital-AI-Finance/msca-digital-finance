### Swedbank AB

### General Description

Swedbank AB is a Nordic-Baltic banking group based in Stockholm, Sweden, offering retail banking, asset management, financial, and other services. One of key markets where Swedbank AB is operating is the Baltics, with which doctoral network activities would take place.

### Our Contribution to the MSCA

Swedbank AB contributes data, training on retail banking, and expertise in asset management and other financial services, covering Northern Europe. Furthermore, Swedbank AB is hosting ESRs and transferable skills training as well as research.
